My Journey and Credentials

The Energy Holistic Health Method was born out of my own journey back to health. Through holistic ideologies, I transformed my body and mind-while allowing my spirit to shine. I was raised on the traditional quick-and-easy of the time- whoppers, crystal light, big breakfast, skittles, and margarine. I seemed spunky and well adjusted- was energetic with lots of friends. Then came the cascade of health concerns ranging from colic inertia and hypoglycemia to infertility and cancer. Humans are made of a system of systems and all mine were shutting down. Following the allopathic route, I found myself depressed and suicidal but it is the darkest, they say, right before the dawn. I changed my career path and began working as a personal trainer- eventually opening a full service gym. To the degree I healed myself – I was allowed to help guide others. My training, at that time, was concerned primarily with sculpting the physical body. I was educated in the best western practices and exercise theories. I got results but in many cases the clients would fall back into their old unhealthy patterns after a period of time. I then discovered the power of real food and got extensive nutritional knowledge. I followed the practices of the Weston A. Price Foundation, Dr. Price & Pottenger, Natasha Campbell-Mcbride, Paul Chek, and Sally Fallon. I had my life’s passion but I wanted to learn more. I began educating myself on the psychological side of health and truly believe the root issue with our disease is NOT found on waistlines but between our ears. Over time I was blessed with wisdom from elders and respected mentors. A teacher must never stop learning. As I studied, patterns emerged and I become captivated with the eastern world’s version of wellness - immersing myself in the practices. Eastern methodologies respect the mind and spirit as well as the body. It is more restorative and holistic. As I grew in my own health journey, I adapted my healing philosophy to reflect my new knowledge and experiences. Thus was born The Energy Holistic Health Method, a respectful blending of eastern and western practices that focus on training the mind, body, and spirit through all the lifecycles.

Being proactive in finding the source of the individual’s problem, where it came from, and how to treat it is the key. The human body has the ability to heal itself from anything-but it needs the tools and a belief in the process. You cannot achieve long-term health and vitality through the management of symptoms (a pill for an ill doesn’t work). Changing the lifestyle, mind-body-spirit, is the key to long-term health and vitality.
